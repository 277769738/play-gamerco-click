About Classic Survivors

Classic Survivors brings you the original survival gameplay experience that inspired a generation of games. In this challenging adventure, you'll need to dodge endless waves of enemies while collecting experience points to grow stronger.

How to Play

Move your character using WASD or arrow keys to avoid enemies. Collect experience gems from defeated enemies to level up. Each level up allows you to choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones. Survive as long as possible while creating powerful ability combinations!
