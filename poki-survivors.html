About Poki Survivors

Poki Survivors takes the survival genre to new heights with its fast-paced action and strategic gameplay. Face off against endless waves of enemies while developing your character into an unstoppable force through clever ability combinations and quick thinking.

How to Play

Control your character with WASD or arrow keys to navigate the battlefield. Your character attacks automatically, so focus on positioning and movement. Collect experience orbs to level up and choose new abilities. Combine different abilities to create powerful synergies and survive longer!
