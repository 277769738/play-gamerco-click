About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an exciting survival game where you must stay alive as long as possible while facing waves of increasingly difficult monsters. Collect experience points to level up, choose powerful abilities, and create the ultimate character build to survive the onslaught.

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Automatically attack nearby enemies and collect experience gems to level up. Choose new abilities and upgrades as you progress to become stronger and survive longer.
